Terrestrial Imaging

In 2017, I began my professional journey with Terrestrial Imaging. A business that I founded with my father. Throughout my 7 years as a business owner and pioneer in the emerging drone industry, I developed countless business and technical skills. My responsibilities ranged from the oversight of sales and marketing activities, to 3D design and 3D printing. Below are some of the many projects I've worked on.

DroMight Strobe Lights

DroMight Strobe Ligths to this day are a popular item for drone users that help them comply with FAA regulations for night flight. I designed the plastic housing with snap fit locks with unique mounting clips for a variety of drone models. I created the design using Sketchup and 3D printed them using Cura slicer software.

Talon Drop System

DroMight Strobe Ligths to this day are a popular item for drone users that help them comply with FAA regulations for night flight. I designed the plastic housing with snap fit locks with unique mounting clips for the different drone models. I created the design using Sketchup and 3D printed them using Cura slicer software and our 3D printers.

Content Creation

Client Training

I've developed training curriculum and have personally trained hundreds of customers on the use and saftey of their drone equipment, and the use of their drone related software.
